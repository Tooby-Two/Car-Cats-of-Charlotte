Car Cats

Cats based off of NASCAR schemes and drivers populate a supernatural world.

Summary

Car Cats are a unique species of feral cat. Evolved for high dexterity, intelligence, and communication, they live lives that mirror the complexity of human society—from racing and triathlons to music, politics, and supernatural legends.

Instead of large nations, the world is shaped by powerful city-states—each with its own culture, power, and secrets. The Car Cat story focuses on two major city-states: Charlotte and Daytona.

Charlotte

A tech-driven metropolis known for its sleek infrastructure, business power, and deep connection to motorsports. Life here is fast-paced, sharp-edged, and status-focused.

Main Characters

  • Creed – A multi-talented student unsure where he fits in a competitive world.
  • Heim – A fierce, fashion-forward cat dreaming of becoming a werewolf hunter.
  • SVK – A cheerful immigrant with surprising depth and determination.
